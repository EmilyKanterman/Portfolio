Assistant Lease Manager

In summer 2018, I intered at Jericho Taxi Brokers as an Assistant Lease Manager. I used software called open data in which I generated reports based on specific dates and times the taxi's were being driven by our drivers. I looked at data on which cabs were in highest usage on certain holidays, for example, and learned to draw valuable conclusions based on these results. Specifically, I got to look at New Years Eve data across different years. Data showed that in certain years, it is best to have drivers stay in New York City because it is a very busy night, which means lots of business, but other years, the cars lose more by staying in the city because of repairs that go into vandalism.
